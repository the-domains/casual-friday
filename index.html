AIDAprima Cruise Ship Construction & Christening in 4K by MK timelapse

MK timelapse GmbH / YouTube
Witness the construction process of AIDA Cruises' new flagship in Nagasaki: AIDAprima, which was recently christened on the Elbe on Saturday the 7th of May 2016, during the Hamburg Hafengeburtstag (harbour birthday) festival. Four MKtimelapse camera systems were recording non-stop at the Koyagi Plant of Mitsubishi Heavy Industries to deliver the world's first cinematic quality time lapse film of a full cruise ship build.
